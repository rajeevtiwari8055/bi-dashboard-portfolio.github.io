Privacy Policy

Voluntarily shared information is used to respond to queries, facilitate learning, or provide guidance. It is not used for marketing or analytics purposes.

All dashboards are created using sample, dummy, or publicly available datasets. No user or visitor data is collected or analyzed.

The website may include links to GitHub, LinkedIn, or other professional platforms. Rajeev is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites. Users should review third-party policies independently.

5. Data Security

Reasonable measures are in place to protect voluntarily shared information. However, no system is completely secure. Users should exercise caution when sharing personal data.

6. No Cookies & Tracking

No cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics are used. Visitors can navigate freely without automated data collection.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use signifies acceptance of any changes. Users are encouraged to review the policy regularly.

8. Contact

Visitors may contact Rajeev via email, LinkedIn, or website contact links for privacy-related questions.
